Control theory is a field of control engineering and applied mathematics that deals with 
the control of dynamical systems in engineered processes and machines. The objective is 
to develop a model or algorithm governing the application of system inputs to drive 
the system to a desired state, while minimizing any delay, overshoot, or steady-state error
and ensuring a level of control stability; often with the aim to achieve a degree 
of optimality.
